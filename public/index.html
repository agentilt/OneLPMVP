The problem

LP teams are stuck in GP portals, spreadsheets, and email attachments — making it easy to miss capital call deadlines, lose documents, and overlook concentration risk.

Our approach

One secure workspace with fund and direct investment data, capital call and distribution tracking, forecasting, risk guardrails, compliance documents, and exports that your board and auditors can use immediately.

Platform pillars LPs rely on

Multi-asset coverage, capital call discipline, risk management, and reporting in one login — already live in the production portal.

Unified portfolio intelligence

Funds and direct investments in one place with NAV, TVPI/DPI, and allocation views by manager, geography, and asset class.

Capital calls & cash flow

Pending call alerts, waterfall and cumulative cash flow charts, distribution timelines, and MOIC visibility across the portfolio.

Risk & policy guardrails

Risk scoring, concentration alerts, stress scenarios (-15%, -30%, -50%), and liquidity/VaR analysis with unfunded commitment forecasts.

Report builder & exports

Drag-and-drop reporting with templates plus PDF, Excel, and CSV exports for investment committees, boards, and auditors.

Workflows built for LP operations

Every module respects fund-level permissions, client scoping, and the day-to-day tasks of LP, advisor, and co-investor teams.

Fund & document workspace

NAV trends with charts, capital call statuses, compliance and tax packets, and a secure download proxy — all searchable via Cmd+K.

Direct investment coverage

Private equity, credit, real estate, public equity, and cash positions with revenue/ARR metrics, valuations, and exportable tables.

Analytics hub & forecasting

Quick insights for NAV, TVPI, unfunded, capital call vs. distribution timelines, saved forecasts by scenario, and liquidity planning.

Access controls & data manager

Relationship types (LP, advisor, co-investor), permission levels, client-level scoping, and searchable user + fund access management.

A unified command center for every LP workflow

Dashboards bring together fund and direct investment cards, cash flow waterfalls, risk scores, policy violations, and capital call timelines so you can respond before issues surface.
